Problem ID 102968

a$)$ An apple is heavier than a banana, and a banana is heavier than a kiwi. What's heavier - a kiwi or an apple?

b$)$ A mandarin is lighter than a pear, and an orange is heavier than a mandarin. What is heavier - a pear or an orange?

Hint

b$)$ Think about whether there is enough information in the task.

Solution

a$)$ Naturally, the apple is heavier than kiwi.

b$)$ There is not enough information in the task to solve it: under the given conditions, an orange can be heavier than a pear, and a pear may be heavier than an orange.
