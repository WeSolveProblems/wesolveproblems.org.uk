Problem ID 89943

What is there a greater number of: cats, except for those cats that are not named Fluffy, or animals named Fluffy, except for those that are not cats?

Hint

Think about who it is - "cats, except those cats that are not called Fluffy"?

Solution

"Cats, except those cats that are not called Fluffy" - these are the cats that are called Fluffy. "Animals named Fluffy, except those who are not cats" - this is also cats named Fluffy, i.e. the same. So, their number is the same.
