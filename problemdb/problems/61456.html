Problem ID 61456

Let f $($x, y$)$ be a harmonic function $($see the definition in Problem 11.28$)$. Prove that the functions $\Delta_{x}$ f $($x, y$)$ = f $($x + 1, y$)$ - f $($x, y$)$ and $\Delta_{y}$ f $($x , y$)$ = f $($x, y + 1$)$ - f $($x, y$)$ will also be harmonic.

Solution

The harmony of these functions is verified by definition.
