Problem WSP009

Is it possible to arrange 44 marbles into 9 piles, such that the number of marbles in each pile is different?

Hint

Think about how many marbles are needed in order to fulfill the conditions of the problem.

Solution

Assume that it is possible. If we arrange the piles in order of increasing size, the first pile must contain at least 1 marble, the second at least 2, the third at least 3, and so on. Therefore, the total number of marbles must be at least $ 1 + 2 + 3 + ... + 9 = 45 $ . But we have only 44 - therefore this is a contradiction.

Answer

No.
