Problem ID 30324

In a football team $($made up of 11 people$)$, a captain and his deputy need to be chosen. How many ways can this be done?

Solution

Any of the 11 players can become the captain. After choosing the captain, the role of his deputy can be chosen from the 10 remaining people. Thus, altogether there are $11 \times 10$ = 110 different options.

Answer

110 ways.
