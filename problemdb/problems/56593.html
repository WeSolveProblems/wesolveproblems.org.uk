Problem ID 56593

On the circle, the points A, B, C and D are given. The lines AB and CD intersect at the point M. Prove that AC \times AD / AM = $BC \times BD / BM$.

Solution

Since $\triangle$ADM $\sim\triangle$CBM and $\triangle$ACM $\sim\triangle$DBM, AD: CB = DM: BM and AC: DB = AM: DM. What remains is to multiply these equalities.
