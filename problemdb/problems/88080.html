Problem ID 88080

What word is encrypted: 22212221265121? Each letter is replaced by its number in the English alphabet.

Hint

Note: the first letter is either B or V.

Solution

Yes, he will be able to.

To solve this problem, we write down the letters with the numbers 1, 2, 5, 6, 12, 21, 22, 26. These will be, respectively, "A", "B", “E”, “F”, “L”, “U”, “V”, “Z”. Now let's look at the options. The first letter is either "B" or "V". In the first case, further variants of "BBBA", "BBU", "BVA", "BBU", "BBUL", "BVL" are possible. No English word begins with such letters. Hence, the first letter in the word will be "V". Let's continue. Further, the letter combinations can now be "VBUB", "VBAB", “VBAV", "VUBB", "VUVU", "VUVA". Among these combinations, only "VUVU" give hope for finding the desired word. Continuing the search, we get the only possible answer: "VUVUZELA".

Answer

VUVUZELA
