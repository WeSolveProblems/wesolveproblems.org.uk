Problem ID 57583

The point D lies on the base AC of the isosceles triangle ABC. Prove that the radii of the circumscribed circles of the triangles ABD and CBD are equal.

Solution

The radii of the circumscribed circles of the triangles ABD and CBD are equal to AB / 2 sin ADB and BC / 2 sin BDC. It remains to note that AB = BC and sin ADB = sin BDC.
