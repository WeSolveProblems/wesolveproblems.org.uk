Problem ID 35339

On the planet Tau Ceti, the landmass takes up more than half the surface area. Prove that the Tau Cetians can drill a hole through the centre of their planet that connects land to land.

Hint

Imagine a projection of the land relative to the centre of the planet.

Solution

The surface occupied by the land and the antipodal surface - that is, directly opposite - to the land overlap. If they did not then the land would take up less than half of the planet's surface area.
