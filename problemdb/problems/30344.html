Problem ID 30344

On a shelf, there are five books. How many ways can you put a few of them in a stack $($a stack can consist of one book$)$?

Solution

Nothing to translate.

Answer

5 + $5 \times 4$ + $5 \times 4 \times 3$ + $2 \times 5!$ = 325 ways.
