Problem ID 98159

A numerical sequence is defined by the following conditions: How many complete squares are found among the first members of this sequence, not exceeding 1,000,000?

Solution

According to the solution of problem number 98152, all complete squares in this sequence have the form $4^m$. There are exactly 10 $(4^{10} = 1024^2 > 10^6)$ numbers of this kind within the specified limits.

6 points.
