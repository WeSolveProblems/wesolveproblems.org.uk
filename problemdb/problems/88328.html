Problem ID 88328

Professions of family members. In the Semenov family there are 5 people: a husband, a wife, their son, a husband's sister and the father of his wife. They all work. One is an engineer, another is a lawyer, the third is a mechanic, the fourth is an economist, the fifth is a teacher. Here's what else is known about them. The lawyer and the teacher are not blood relatives. The mechanic is a good athlete. He followed in the footsteps of an economist and played football for the national team of the plant. The engineer is older than his brother's wife, but younger than the teacher. The economist is older than the mechanic. What are the professions of each member of the Semenov family?

Solution

We rewrite the condition of the problem by numbering the data. 1$)$ The lawyer and the teacher are not blood relatives. 2$)$ the mechanic is a good athlete. 3$)$ He went in the footsteps of an economist and played football for the national team of the plant. 4$)$ The engineer is older than the wife of his brother, but younger than the teacher. 5$)$ The economist is older than the mechanic. What are the professions of each member of the Semenov family? The mechanic is of a masculine gender. The sister is older than the brother 4$)$, therefore, the son is the youngest, therefore, he is not an economist. The oldest is the teacher, therefore, he is the father of the wife, but then $($ from 3 $)$ the father of the son is an economist, therefore the son is a mechanic.

Answer

The husband is a lawyer, the wife is an economist, the son is a mechanic, the sister of the husband is an engineer, the father of the wife is a teacher.
