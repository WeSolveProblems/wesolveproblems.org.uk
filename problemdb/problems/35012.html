Problem ID 35012

It is known that 35! = 10333147966386144929 * 66651337523200000000. Find the number replaced by an asterisk.

Hint

The number 35! is divisible by 9.

Solution

Because 35! is divisible by 9, the sum of the digits of this number is also divisible by 9. It is easy to calculate that the sum of the digits $($with the exception of the asterisk$)$ of the written number gives the remainder 3 when divided by 9. It follows that the number replaced by an asterisk is 6.

Answer

6.
