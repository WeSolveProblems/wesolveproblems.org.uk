Problem ID 35238

A Cartesian plane is coloured in in two colours. Prove that there will be two points on the plane that are a distance of 1 apart and are the same colour.

Hint

Consider an equilateral triangle with side length 1.

Solution

Consider an equilateral triangle with side length 1. By the pigeon hole principle, at least two of its vertices must be the same colour.
