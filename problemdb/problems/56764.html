Problem ID 56764

The diagonals of the quadrilateral ABCD intersect at the point O. Prove that SAOB = SCOD if and only if BC || AD.

Solution

If SAOB = SCOD, then $AO \times BO$ = $CO \times DO$. Therefore $\triangle$ AOD $\sim$ $\triangle$ COB and AD || BC. These arguments are reversible.
