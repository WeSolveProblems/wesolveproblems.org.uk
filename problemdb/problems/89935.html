Problem ID 89935

On an island, there are knights who always tell the truth, and liars who always lie. What question would you need to ask the islander to find out if he has a crocodile at home?

Hint

Pay attention, if a liar is forced to repeat his answer, then it will already be true.

Solution

It is enough to ask, for example, "What would you say to me a minute ago if I asked if you have a green crocodile at home?". When answering such a question, the knight truthfully replies the true answer, and the liar lies, retelling the false answer, i.e. he will answer with the truth.
