Problem ID 79570

In a group of seven boys, everyone has at least three brothers who are in that group. Prove that all seven are brothers.

Solution

Take any two boys from this group. Suppose they are not brothers. Then each of them has three brothers among the remaining five boys. Therefore, they have a common brother, which means they are brothers. So, following this logic, any two boys from this group are brothers.
