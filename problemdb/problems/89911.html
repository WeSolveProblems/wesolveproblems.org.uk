Problem ID 89911

The tower clock chimes three times in 12 seconds. How long will six chimes last?

Hint

What is the interval between two consecutive chimes?

Solution

Since 3 chimes of the clock occur in 12 seconds, the interval between two consecutive strokes is 6 seconds. From the first chime to the second - 6 seconds and from the second to the third - also 6 seconds. Six chimes are distributed with 5 intervals. Consequently, 6 chimes of the clock will strike for $5 \time 6$ = 30 seconds.
