Problem ID 53431

The angles of a triangle are in the ratio 2: 3: 4. Find the ratio of the outer angles of the triangle.

Solution

Since the outer angle is equal to the sum of two internal angles, the outer angles are in the ratio $($2 + 3$)$: $($2 + 4$)$: $($3 + 4$)$.

Answer

5:6:7.
