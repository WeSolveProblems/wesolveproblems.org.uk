Problem ID 57480

ABC is a right angled triangle with a right angle at C. Prove that $c^n$ $>$ $a^n$ + $b^n$ for n> 2.

Solution

Since $c^2$ = $a^2$ + $b^2$, then $c^n$ = $($a^2$ + $b^2$)$ $c^{n-2}$ = $a^2c^{n-2}$ + $b^2c^{n-2}$ $>$ $a^n$ + $b^n$.
