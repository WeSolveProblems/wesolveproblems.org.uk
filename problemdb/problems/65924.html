Problem ID 65924

Replace the letters with numbers $($ all digits must be different $)$ so that the correct equality is obtained: A/ B/ C + D/ E/ F + G/ H/ I = 1.

Answer

For example, 2/ 1/ 4 + 9/ 3/ 6 + 0/ 5/ 7 = 1 or 5/ 4/ 3 + 7/ 6/ 2 + 0/ 8/ 9 = 1.

Remarks

There are many other examples in which one of the terms is equal to zero, and also only one example in which all terms are nonzero: 1/ 3/ 6 + 5/ 8/ 9 + 7/ 2/ 4 = 1.
