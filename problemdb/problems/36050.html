Problem ID 36050

Andrew drives his car at a speed of 60 km/h. He wants to travel every kilometre 1 minute faster. By how much should he increase his speed?

Solution

Andrew spends only one minute of time on each kilometre, so it will not work to travel every kilometre one minute faster.

Answer

He will not be able to do this.
