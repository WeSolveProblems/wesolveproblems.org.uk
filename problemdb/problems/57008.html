Problem ID 57008

a) Prove that the sum of the angles at the vertices of a convex n-gon is $(n - 2) \times 180$ $^{\circ}$. b) The convex n-gon is cut by diagonals that do not cross to form triangles. Prove that the number of these triangles is equal to n - 2.
