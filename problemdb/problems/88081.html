Problem ID 88081

Write the first 10 prime numbers in a line. How can you remove 6 digits to get the largest possible number?

Hint

Notice that the first 10 prime numbers will be a 16-digit number.

Solution

Here are the first ten primes written in succession: 2357111317192329

Answer

The result is the number 7317192329.
