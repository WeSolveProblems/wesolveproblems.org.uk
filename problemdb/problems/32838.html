Problem ID 32838

Harry and Matt came down from a mountain. Harry walked on foot, and Matt went skiing, which was seven times faster than Harry. Halfway down, Matt fell, broke his skis and his leg, and hence travelled twice as slow as Harry. Who will descend first from the mountain?

Solution

Matt walked on foot from halfway up the mountain for as long as Harry descended from the summit.

Answer

Harry.

Remarks

1. In fact, Harry will catch up with Matt and help him, so they will come together.

2. Compare with the tasks 36043, 36044, 88128.
