Problem ID 89928

In a purse there are 2 coins which make a total of 15 pence. One of them is not a five pence coin. What kind of coins are these?

Solution

The two coins can only be a 5 pence coin and a 10 pence coin. If one of them is not a 5 pence coin, then it is a a ten pence coin, and the other is a 5 pence coin.
