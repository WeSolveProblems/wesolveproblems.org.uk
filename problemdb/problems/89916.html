Problem ID 89916

How can we explain why the answers in problems 89914 and 89915 are different?

Hint

Pay attention, that from a bagel "to make" a sector, one cut is required.

Solution

When a bagel is cut into pieces, the number of cuts and the number of sectors coincide, since one cut is needed in order to "make" a sector from the bagel.
