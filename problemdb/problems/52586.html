Problem ID 52586

A circle is divided up by the points A, B, C, D so that $\smile{AB}$:$\smile{BC}$:$\smile{CD}$:$\smile{DA} = 2: 3: 5: 6. The chords AC and BD intersect at point M. Find the angle AMB.

Solution

Answer

78$^{\circ}$ 45'.
