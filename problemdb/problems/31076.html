Problem ID 31076

Can the degrees of vertices in the graph be equal to:

a) 8, 6, 5, 4, 4, 3, 2, 2?

b) 7, 7, 6, 5, 4, 2, 2, 1?

c) 6, 6, 6, 5, 5, 3, 2, 2?

Solution

a) In a graph of eight vertices, the degree of 8 cannot occur.

b) If there are two vertices that are connected with all other vertices, then the degree of each of the remaining vertices is at least 2.

c) The sum of the degrees of vertices must be even.

Answer

a)-c) No, they cannot.
