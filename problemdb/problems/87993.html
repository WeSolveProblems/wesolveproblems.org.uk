Problem ID 87993

Replace the question marks with the appropriate letters or words:

a$)$ r, o, y, g, b, ?, ?;

b$)$ a, c, f, j, ?, ?;

c$)$ one, three, five, ?,

d$)$ A, H, I, M, O, T, U, ?, ?, ?, ?;

e$)$ o, t, t, f, f, s, s, e, ?, ?;

Hint

Usually, when the pattern is searched for is in letters, either these are the first letters of words, or the numbers of letters in the alphabet. There are, of course, other patterns.

Solution

a$)$ i, v. Written are the first letters of the colors of the rainbow. b$)$ o, u, One letter of the alphabet is written down, one is skipped, two letters are written down, two are skipped, and so on. c$)$ four. The number of letters in the previous word is written. d$)$ V, W, X, Y. The letters with a vertical axis of symmetry are written. e$)$ n, t. The first letters of the numbers of the natural series are written.

Answer

a$)$ i, v; b$)$ o, u; c$)$ four; d$)$ V, W, X, Y; e$)$ n, t;
