Problem ID 87954

What is the maximum number of pieces that a round pancake can be divided into with three straight cuts?

Hint

Remember task 11.

Decision

If out of three straight lines every two intersect inside the pancake, you will get 7 pieces $($see Figure 11.4$)$. If from these straight lines two are parallel or intersect outside the pancake, then the number of pieces will be lower.

Answer

7 pieces.
