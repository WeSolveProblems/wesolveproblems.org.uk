Problem ID 31088

In a country, each two cities are connected with a one-way road. Prove that there is a city from which you can drive to any other whilst travelling along no more than two roads.

Hint

This is the city from which the largest number of roads come out.

Solution

Consider the city A, from which the largest number of roads come out, and an arbitrary city B. If there is a road which leads from A to B, then everything is in order. If the road leads from B to A, then since B has no more roads coming out of it than A does, then there is a city C, into which the road from A leads, but to which the road from B does not lead. Then you can get from A to B along the route ACB.
