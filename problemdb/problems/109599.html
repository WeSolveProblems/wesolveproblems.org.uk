Problem ID 109599

Author: A.S. Golovanov

Prove that for any natural number $a_1> 1$ there exists an increasing sequence of natural numbers $a_1, a_2, a_3$, ..., for which $a_1^2+ a_2^2 +…+ a_k^2$ is divisible by $a_1+ a_2+…+ a_k$ for all k ≥ 1.

Solution
