Problem ID 61422

a) The Young diagrams $($4, 1, 1$)$ and $($3, 3, 0$)$ are not comparable, neither of them majorises the other. Are there any other incomparable sets with a sum of 6?

b) Find all of the incomparable pairs of sets for s = 7.

See here about Young diagrams.

Solution

Nothing to translate.

Answer

a) There are no others. b) $($5, 1, 1$)$ and $($4, 3, 0$)$; $($4, 1, 1, 1$)$ and $($3, 3, 1, 0$)$; $($3, 1, 1, 1, 1$)$ and $($2, 2, 2, 1, 0$)$.
