Problem ID 60408

Why are the equalities $11^2$ = 121 and $11^3$ = 1331 similar to the lines of Pascal's triangle? What is $11^4$ equal to?

Solution

$11^2$ = $(10 + 1)^2$ = $100 + 2 \times 10 + 1, 11^3$ = $(10 + 1)^3$ = 1000 + $3 \times 100 + 3 \times 10 + 1$.

$11^4$ = $C_4^0 \times 10^4$ = $C_4^1 \times 10^3$ + $C_4^2 \times 10^2$ + $C_4^3 \times 10$ + $C_4^4 \times 10$.

Answer

$11^4$ = 14641.
