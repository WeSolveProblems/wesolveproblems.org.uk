Problem ID 60463

Write in terms of prime factors the numbers 111, 1111, 11111, 111111, 1111111.

Answer

111 = $3 \times 37$, 1111 = $11 \times 101$, 11111 = $41 \times 271$, 111111 = $3 \times 7 \times 11 \times 13 \times 37$, 1111111 = $239 \times 4649$.
