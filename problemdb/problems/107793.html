Problem ID 107793

Cut the interval [-1, 1] into black and white segments so that the integrals of any a$)$ linear function; b$)$ a square trinomial in white and black segments are equal.

Solution

Since the integral of a polynomial over a segment is equal to the increment of its antiderivative, our problem is a particular case of problem number 98268.

Answer

For example, a$)$ the segments [-1, -1/2], [1/2, 1] are black, [-1/2, 1/2] are white. b$)$ the segments [-1, - 3/], [- 1/4, 0], [1/4, 3/4] are white, [- 3/4, - 1/4], [0, 1/4], [3/4, 1] are black.
