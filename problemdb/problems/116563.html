Problem ID 116563

Does there exist a real number α such that the number cos α is irrational, and all the numbers cos 2α, cos 3α, cos 4α, cos 5α are rational?

Solution

Suppose the contrary. Then the number A = cos α + cos 5α is irrational, as the sum of a rational and an irrational number is irrational. On the other hand, A = 2 cos 2α cos 3α which is rational as the product of three rational numbers is rational. This gives us a contradiction.

Answer

No there does not exist.

Remarks

If we remove cos 5α from the condition, then the

Answer

will be different. For example, for α = π / 6, the number cos α is irrational, and the numbers cos 2α, cos 3α, cos 4α are rational. On the other hand, the solution shows that cos 4α can be removed from the condition painlessly.
