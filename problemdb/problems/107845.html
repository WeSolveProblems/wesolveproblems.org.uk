Problem ID 107845

Is it possible to find natural numbers $x$, $y$ and $z$ which satisfy the equation $28x+30y+31z=365$?

Solution

In a year there are 12 months. One of these (February) has 28 days. Four months (April, June, September, November) have 30 days and the rest of the months have 31 days. Since there are 365 days in a year, so we have: $28\times 1 + 30\times 4 + 31\times 7 = 365$.

Answer

Yes such $x$, $y$ and $z$ can be found.

Remark

There is also another solution: $x=2$, $y=1$, $z=9$.
