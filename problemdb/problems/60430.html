Problem ID 60430

There are three boxes, in each of which there are balls numbered from 0 to 9. One ball is taken from each box. What is the probability that

a$)$ three ones were taken out;

b$)$ three equal numbers were taken out?

Answer

a$)$ 1/$10^3$; b$)$ 1/$10^2$.
