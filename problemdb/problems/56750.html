Problem ID 56750

Let $A_1, B_1, C_1$ and $D_1$ be the midpoints of the sides CD, DA, AB, BC of the square ABCD, whose area is equal to S. Find the area of the quadrangle formed by the straight lines $AA_1, BB1_, CC_1$ and $DD_1$.
