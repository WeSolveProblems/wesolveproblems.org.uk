Problem ID 57335

A triangle of area 1 with sides a $\leq$ b $\leq$ c is given. Prove that b $\geq$ $\sqrt{2}$.

Solution

It is clear that 2 = 2S = ab sin $\gamma$ $\leq$ ab $\leq$ $b^2$, that is, b $\geq$ $\sqrt{2}$.
