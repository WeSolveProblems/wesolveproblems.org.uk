Problem ID 64638

The numbers x, y and z are such that all three numbers x + yz, y + zx and z + xy are rational, and $x^2$ + $y^2$ = 1. Prove that the number $xyz^2$ is also rational.

Solution

$xyz^2$ = $xyz^2$ + z - z = $xyz^2$ + $($x^2 + y^2$)$ z - z = $($yz + x$)$ $($zx + y$)$ - $($xy + z$)$.

Remarks
