Problem ID 57211

Construct a triangle with the side c, median to side a, $m_a$, and median to side b, $m_b$.

Solution

Suppose that the triangle ABC is constructed. Let M be the point of intersection of medians $AA_1$ and $BB_1$. Then AM = $2m_a$ / 3 and BM = $2m_b$ / 3. The triangle ABM can be constructed along the lengths of the sides AB = c, AM and BM. Then, on the rays AM and BM, we mark the segments $AA_1$ = $m_a$ and $BB_14 = $m_b4.
