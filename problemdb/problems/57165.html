Problem ID 57165

Let O be the center of the rectangle ABCD. Find the geometric points of M for which AM $\geq$ OM, BM $\geq$ OM, CM $\geq$ OM, and DM $\geq$ OM.

Solution

Draw the middle perpendicular l to the segment AO. It is clear that AM $\geq$ OM if and only if the point M lies on the same side of the line l as the point O $($or lies on the line l$)$. Therefore, the sought-for geometric points are a rhombus formed by the middle perpendiculars to the segments OA, OB, OC and OD.
