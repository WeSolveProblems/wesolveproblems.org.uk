Problem ID 61175

Let $z_1$ and $z_2$ be fixed points of a complex plane. Give a geometric description of the sets of all points z that satisfy the conditions: a) arg = 0; b) arg = 0.

Answer

a) The part of the straight line passing through the points $z_1$ and $z_2$, located outside of the segment [$z_1, z_2$].

b) The interior of the segment joining the points $z_1$ and $z_2$.
