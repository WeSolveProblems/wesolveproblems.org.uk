Problem ID 57584

Express the area of the triangle ABC through the length of the side BC and the angles B and C.

Solution

By the sine theorem, b = a sin $\beta$ / sin $\alpha$ = a sin $\beta$ / sin $(\beta + \gamma)$, so S = ab sin $\gamma$ / 2 = $a^2sin \beta sin \gamma / 2 sin (\beta + \gamma)$.
