Problem ID 89919

Are the sum and product odd or even for:

a$)$ two even numbers?

b$)$ two odd numbers?

c$)$ an odd and an even number?

Answer

a$)$ Even; b$)$ the sum is even, the product is odd; c$)$ the sum is odd, the product is even.
