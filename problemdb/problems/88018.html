Problem ID 88018

On a table five coins are placed in a row: the middle coin shows heads and the rest show tails. It is allowed to turn over three adjacent coins simultaneously. Is it possible to get all five coins to show heads after turning the coins over several times?

Hint

Try turning over the first three coins.

Solution

It can be done, and pretty quickly. We will turn over the first three coins. Then the first two coins will show heads, and the last three will show tails. Now we turn over the last three coins, and all five coins will show heads.

Answer

Yes: in the first turn, we turn over the first 3 coins, the second - the last 3 coins.
