Problem ID 89925

One person says: "I'm a liar." Is he a native of the island of knights and liars?

Hint

Think about whether this person can be a knight.

Solution

Both the knight and the liar will both say about themselves: "I am a knight." Therefore, this person is not an inhabitant of the Island.
