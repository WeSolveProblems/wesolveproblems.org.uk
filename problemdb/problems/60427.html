Problem ID 60427

Find the sums of the following series:

a) $ {\frac {1} {1 \times 2}} $ + $ {\frac {1} {2 \times 3}} $ + $ {\frac {1} {3 \times 4}} $ + $ {\frac {1} {4 \times 5}} $ + ...;

b) $ {\frac {1} {1 \times 2 \times 3}} $ + $ {\frac {1} {2 \times 3 \times 4}} $ + $ {\frac {1} {3 \times 4 \times 5}} $ + $ {\frac {1} {4 \times 5 \times 6}} $ + ...;

c) $ {\frac {0!} {r!}} $ + $ {\frac {1!} {(r-1)!}} $ + $ {\frac {2!} {(r-2) !}} $ + $ {\frac {3!} {(r-3)!}} $ + ... (r $ \geq $ 2).

Solution

Nothing to translate.

Answer

a) 1.

b) Answer missing.

c) $ {\frac {1} {(r-1)! \times (r-1)}} $.
