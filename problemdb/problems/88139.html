Problem ID 88139

Try to make a square from a set of rods:

6 rods of length 1 cm, 3 rods of length 2 cm each, 6 rods of length 3 cm and 5 rods of length 4 cm. You are not able to break the rods or place them on top of one another.

Hint

Try to determine the length of the side of the desired square.

Solution

Such a square cannot be made, because its perimeter should be 50 cm, i.e. the sides are not integers.

Answer

This cannot be done.
