Problem ID 88128

Pinocchio and Pierrot were racing. Pierrot ran the entire race at the same speed, and Pinocchio ran half the way two times faster than Pierrot, and the second half twice as slow as Pierrot. Who won the race?

Hint

Note that for the second half of the race, Pinocchio spent exactly as much time as Pierrot spent on the entire race.

Solution

For the second half of the race, Pinocchio spent the same amount of time as Pierrot spent on the entire race. But after all, it took some time for Pinocchio run half of the race, so, Pierrot won.

Answer

Pierrot.
