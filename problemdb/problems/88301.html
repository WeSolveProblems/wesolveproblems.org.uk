Problem ID 88301

A cube with a side of 1 m was sawn into cubes with a side of 1 cm and they were in a row $($along a straight line$)$. How long was the line?

Solution

We get $100 \times 100 \times 100$ = 1,000,000 cm or 10,000 m = 10 km.
