Problem ID 88246

Will the quotient or the remainder change if a divided number and the divisor are increased by 3 times?

Solution

The quotient will not change, and the remainder will triple. However, it will not change if it was 0.

Answer

The quotient will not change, the remainder will change if it was not equal to zero.
