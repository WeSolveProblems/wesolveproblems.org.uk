Problem

Is it always the case that in 25 GBP banknotes (That is £5, £10, £20, £50 notes) there will always be 7 banknotes of the same denomination?

Hint

Think about how many banknotes there will be, if there are no more than 6 bankotes of each of the 4 denominations.

Solution

Yes, If each of there were no more than 6 banknotes of each of the 4 denominations, the maximimum number of banknotes is 24. However there are 25, so there must be at least one denomination of which there are 7 banknotes.
