Problem ID 86112

Does there exist a flat quadrilateral in which the tangents of all interior angles are equal?

Solution

This quadrilateral is one with three angles equal to 45$^{\circ}$, and the fourth to 225$^{\circ}$ $($then the tangents of all its angles are equal to 1$)$.

Answer

Yes, such a quadrilateral does exist.
