Problem ID 88161

Find the missing numbers:

Hint

Remember tasks 513, 514, 528.

Solution

$($a$)$ 71; To get the next number, multiply the previous by 2 and subtract the sequence number of the previous number. b$)$ 17; to get the next number, multiply the previous by 2 and subtract 1. c$)$ 11 and 14; on even places there is a sequence of 10, 11, 12, 13, ..., and at odd places - a sequence of 8, 9, 10, .... d$)$ 17; each next number is equal to the sum of the two previous ones. Answer $($a$)$ 71; b$)$ 17; c$)$ 11 and 14; d$)$ 17
