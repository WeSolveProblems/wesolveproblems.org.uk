Problem ID 88071

Find all of the natural numbers that, when divided by 7, have the same remainder and quotient.

Hint

When dividing a number by 7, only 7 different remainders are possible.

Solution

The remainder when dividing by 7 cannot exceed 6, so the numbers that are of interest to us can be represented as 7a + a = 8a, where a = 1, 2, ..., 6. So, these numbers are: 8, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48.

Answer

8, 16, 24, 32, 40, 48.
