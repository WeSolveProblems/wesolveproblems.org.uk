Problem ID 32830

During the year, the price for a strudel were twice raised by 50%, and before the New Year they were sold at half price. How much does one strudel cost now, if at the beginning of the year it cost 80 pence?

Solution

After the first increase in prices, the strudel began to cost $80 \times 1.5$ = 120 pence; after the second price increase - $120 \times 1.5$ = 180 pence; finally, after a twofold reduction in the price, it began to cost 180/2 = 90 pence.

Answer

90 pence.
