Problem ID 21993

The numbers $1, 2, ..., 9$ are divided into three groups. Prove that the product of the numbers in one of the groups will always be no less than 72.

Solution

The product of all the numbers in all the groups is equal to $9!$, however $71^3=357911< 9!$.
