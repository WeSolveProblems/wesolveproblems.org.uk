Problem

What is the maximum number of kings you could place on a chess board such that no two of them were attacking each other (That is, no two kings are on horizontally, vertically or diagonally adjacent squares).

Solution

16 kings. Divide the chess board into 16 squares, each of which cannot contain more than one king.
