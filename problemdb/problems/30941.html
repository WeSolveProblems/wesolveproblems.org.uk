Problem ID 30941

Every Martian has three hands. Can seven Martians join hands?

Solution

Altogether there are 21 hands, which is an odd number. If they joined hands, their hands would break into pairs $($each pair includes two hands that shake hands with each other$)$, and this is impossible.

Answer

No, they cannot.
