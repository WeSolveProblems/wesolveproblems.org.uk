Problem ID 60431

How many six-digit numbers exist that are divisible by 5?

Solution 1

There are 90000 five-digit numbers $($see the solution of problem number 60336$)$. To each of them, in order to obtain a six-digit number that is a multiple of 5, you need to add a 0 or a 5 at the end.

Solution 2

In every five consecutive numbers, exactly one is multiple of 5. Therefore, the number of six-digit numbers, that are multiples of 5, is five times less than the total number of all six-digit numbers. And there are 900000 of them.

Answer

180000 numbers.
