Problem ID 32050

In a tournament by the Olympic system $($the loser is eliminated$)$, 50 boxers participate. What is the minimum number of matches needed to be conducted in order to identify the winner?

Solution

After each battle, one boxer, the loser in this fight, is eliminated from the competition. Since only by the end of the competition all but the winner will be eliminated, there must be 49 fights, regardless of how the schedule is drawn up.

Answer

49 fights.

Remarks

Source of the solution: V.O. Bugaenko "Lomonosov Tournament Competitions in Mathematics". MCNMO-CheRo. 1998.
