Problem ID 103848

In the line of numbers and signs * 1 * 2 * 4 * 8 * 16 * 32 * 64 = 27 position the signs "+" or "-" instead of the signs "*", so that the equality becomes true.

Hint

You can find the required arrangement of signs easily, if you arrange the signs from right to left.

Try to prove to yourself that

a$)$ for any arrangement of signs on the left side of the equation, an odd number is obtained;

b$)$ the choice of signs can make any odd number between the numbers -128 and 128, and each number can be made in only one way.

Solution

We will replace the asterisks from right to left. Since 1 + 2 + 4 + 8 + 16 + 32 = 63 $<$ 64, before 64 there should be a "+" $($otherwise the result will be negative$)$. Therefore we get 1 * 2 * 4 * 8 * 16 * 32 + 64 = 27. Before 32 there should be a '' - '', otherwise the left side will be too great of a number, even if the rest of the asterisks are replaced by a '' - ''. Continuing in a similar way, we get +1 - 2 + 4 + 8 - 16 - 32 + 64 = 27.

Answer

The signs can be arranged as follows: +1 - 2 + 4 + 8 - 16 - 32 + 64 = 27.
