Problem ID 53435

One angle of a triangle is equal to the sum of its other two angles. Prove that the triangle is right-angled.

Hint

Use the theorem of the sum of the angles of a triangle.

Solution

Let α, β and α + β be the angles of the triangle. Then α + β + $($α + β$)$ = 180$^{\circ}$, whence α + β = 90$^{\circ}$.
