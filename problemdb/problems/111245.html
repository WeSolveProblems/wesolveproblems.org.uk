Problem ID 111245

Let's denote any two digits with the letters A and X. Prove that the six-digit number XAXAXA is divisible by 7 without a remainder.

Solution

XAXAXA = $XA \times 10,000$ + $XA \times 100$ + XA = $XA \times 10101$. Since 10101 = $7 \times 1443$, then XAXAXA is divisible by 7 without a remainder.
