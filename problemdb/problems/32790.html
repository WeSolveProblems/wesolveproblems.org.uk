Problem ID 32790

It is known that among the members of the government of Limonia $($and there are 20 members in all$)$ there is at least one honest one, and also that from any two at least one is a bribe taker. How many bribe takers are there in the government?

Solution

Let us note that in the government of Limonia there is exactly one honest official. Indeed, by the condition of the problem, there is one honest official; but there cannot be two honest officials there - then amongst them there would not be a single bribe taker, which contradicts the condition. So, there is exactly one honest official in the government, and therefore, there are 19 bribe takers.

Solution

There are 19 bribe takers in the government.
