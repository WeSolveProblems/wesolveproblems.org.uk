Problem ID 35543

One and a half diggers dig for a half hour and end up having dug half a pit. How many pits will two diggers dig in two hours?

Solution

Two diggers in 1.5 hours will dig up 4/3 times more, that is 2 pits. And for two hours - another 4/3 times more, that is $2 \times 4/3$ = 8/3 pits.

Answer

22/3 pits.

Remarks

1. Of course, both the condition and the solution do not have any "physical" meaning.

2. Compare with the tasks 103751, 103755.
